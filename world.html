Explore the World

Six vast regions await - each with its own climate, secrets, monsters, and story.

🗺️ The Six Regions

Auryn Plains

Golden meadows and rolling hills. The starting region — perfect for new adventurers.

Verdant Forest

An ancient forest full of glowing plants, hidden temples, and rare creatures.

Searing Sands

A vast desert with scorching heat. Pyro monsters guard ancient ruins and treasure.

Crystalpeak Mountains

Icy peaks covered in permanent snow. Cryo dragons nest here. High-rank only!

Azure Shores

Sparkling coastlines, underwater caves, and floating islands. Sea storms abound!

Stormveil Highlands

Permanently storm-covered highlands. Lightning never stops here.
