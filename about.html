About the Game

Everything you need to know about Lama Legends - how it plays, and what makes it special.

What is Lama Legends?

Lama Legends is a free-to-play, anime-style open-world role playing game (RPG) available on PC, consoles, and mobile. Players can explore a fantasy world full of wonder, danger, and hidden stories.

Build a party of 4 characters with unique elemental abilities, fight monsters, clear dungeons, and take on a world bosses. Levelling your Adventure Rank unlocks new regions and better rewards.

The gacha system lets you pull for limited banner characters and weapons using in-game currency earned just by playing.
